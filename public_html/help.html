Settings

The user will be prompted to configure the settings of a game before beginning game play. The elements that require specification are:

  • Number of Players: the number of players allowed in a single session
  • Number of Decks: the number of decks you would like to play with for a single game.
  • Maximum Bet: the largest amount allowed for any bet.
  • Minimum Bet: the minimum amount required to be bet before a hand can be dealt.
  • Starting Money: the amount of "money" the player(s) will start with.
  • Shuffle At: the amount of cards left in the shoe before the dealer shuffles
  • Insurance: a ratio for what the insurance payout will be (usually 2:1). MUST BE ENTERED AS A DECIMAL.
  • Blackjack Pay: similar to Insurance, however the payout will usually be 3:1 MUST BE ENTERED AS A DECIMAL.
  • H17: indicates whether the dealer will hit on a soft 17
